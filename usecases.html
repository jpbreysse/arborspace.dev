Turn 15 years of consulting expertise into a sovereign competitive advantage.

AI-Driven Support Ticketing with Game Theory Evaluation

Tickets that guide engineers. Conversations scored by strategic outcomes.

A software supplier uses ArborSpace as its support platform. Incoming tickets are auto-classified, the knowledge base suggests solutions to engineers, workflows generate investigation steps, and every closed conversation is evaluated using game-theory scoring — measuring whether both the customer and the support engineer reached an optimal outcome.

NovaSoft provides L2/L3 support for its financial platform

A customer reports a performance degradation in their batch processing module. The ticket flows through auto-classification, AI-guided diagnosis, resolution, and post-resolution evaluation. Game theory scoring ensures the resolution wasn't just fast — it was strategically optimal for both parties.
